Bolides

Instructions

Press space to fire, WASD or arrow keys to move. Asteroids are worth 100 pts, Bolides are worth 500 pts.

Gain points equal to the current level times 100 to advance to the next level and increase the difficulty.

If you touch an asteroid, you will lose a life unless you can correctly answer a question. Lose all your lives, and the game ends.

At the end of the game, your score will be recorded, and you can check the high scorers on the Scoreboard screen.
